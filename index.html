Frequently asked questions

Clarity before you begin.

Who can join the DCBA program?

DCBA is exclusively for career gap candidates and career restarters, including women returning after a break, Government exam aspirants, graduates who never started their careers and professionals returning after an employment gap.

What is the minimum qualification?

Graduation in any discipline and good English communication skills. No coding background or prior IT experience is required.

Can I restart after a long employment gap?

Yes. The structured learning journey, mentor feedback, project portfolio and interview preparation are designed to help you rebuild confidence and explain your transition positively.

Does the program include placement assistance?

Yes. Support covers resume building, profile positioning, mock interviews, opportunity guidance and selection preparation.

Which tools and capabilities are included?

JIRA, Confluence, Microsoft Visio, Balsamiq, Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Microsoft Azure and Product Management.
